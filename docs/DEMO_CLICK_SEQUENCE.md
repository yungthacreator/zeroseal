# ZeroSeal Demo Click Sequence

1. Open `https://zeroseal.vercel.app`.
2. Click **Try ZeroSeal**.
3. Click **Load example**.
4. In **Report**, confirm `Immunefi`, `Example Vault Programme`, `ExampleVault.sol`, `Smart contract` and `withdraw()`.
5. Click **Continue**.
6. In **Finding**, confirm `Critical`, `Access control`, `50,000 USD` public threshold and the public summary.
7. Click **Continue**.
8. In **Private evidence**, confirm private report, reproduction steps, optional PoC notes and private impact value.
9. Click **Continue**.
10. Click **Generate private seal**.
11. Click **Approve public claim**.
12. Click **Continue**.
13. Click **Connect Freighter** if the wallet is not already connected.
14. Confirm the wallet shows **Connected** and **Stellar Testnet**.
15. Click **Review transaction**.
16. Confirm the transaction review says raw evidence is excluded.
17. Click **Approve in Freighter**.
18. Approve in Freighter.
19. Wait for the confirmed transaction hash and ledger.
20. Click **Open receipt page**.
21. Click **Open Stellar explorer**.
22. Copy the transaction hash.
23. Open `/verify`.
24. Paste the transaction hash.
25. Click **Verify receipt**.
